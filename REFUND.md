Last updated: June 2026

## Subscriptions

ClearRates is billed monthly. You may cancel your subscription at any time and will retain access until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are issued for partial months.

## 7-Day Free Trial

New subscribers receive a 7-day free trial. You will not be charged until the trial period ends. Cancel before the trial ends and you will not be charged.

If you believe you have been charged in error, contact us within 14 days at traveltaxdesk@gmail.com and we will review your case. Refunds are issued at our discretion.
