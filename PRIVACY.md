# Privacy Policy — ClearRates

Last updated: May 2026

## What we are

ClearRates is a Model Context Protocol (MCP) server providing UK statutory employment rate lookups to Claude AI users. Operated as an independent service, UK.

## Data we collect

**Account data:** Email address collected when you request access. Payment information is handled by our payment processor — we do not store payment card details.

**Usage data:** We log the number of API requests per licence key for rate limiting purposes. We do not log the specific queries you submit.

**Query data:** Queries are processed in-memory and are not stored or logged by ClearRates.

## How we use data

- To validate your licence key and provide access to the service
- To monitor service health and uptime
- To comply with legal obligations

## Third-party services

- **Render** — server hosting (render.com). Render privacy policy: render.com/privacy

## Data retention

Licence key validation logs are retained for 30 days then deleted. We do not retain query content.

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at the email below.
